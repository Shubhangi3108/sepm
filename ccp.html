Child Care Plus is a digital platform based in Chennai that offers comprehensive child care services.

The platform aims to provide parents with a convenient way to track their child's vaccination schedule and receive reminders for upcoming vaccinations. It also allows parents to search for nearby pediatricians, book appointments, and manage their child's health records.
